## Project Name
PocketMentor AI

## Tagline
Your personalized AI financial coach to master your budget and savings.

## The Problem
Students and young adults often struggle with financial literacy and budgeting. Traditional budgeting apps are overly complex, require manual transaction tagging, and don't actually *teach* users how to improve their habits.

## The Solution
PocketMentor AI takes basic inputs (income, fixed expenses, goals, and behavioral habits) and leverages NVIDIA NIM API (Llama 3 70B) to generate a personalized, actionable financial dashboard. It provides simple spending alerts, a custom savings roadmap, and explains complex financial concepts dynamically based on the user's specific context.
