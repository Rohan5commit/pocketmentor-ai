# Demo Script (3 Minutes)

1. **Introduction (30s)**: "Hi, we are building PocketMentor AI. Most students don't know how to budget, and standard apps are just spreadsheets in disguise. We wanted to build an app that actually *mentors* you."
2. **Onboarding (45s)**: "Here is our landing page. Let's click Get Started. I'm going to enter a typical student profile: $1200 income, $650 expenses, trying to save $200 for a Spring Break trip. For my habits, I'll say 'I eat out a lot, mostly DoorDash'."
3. **AI Generation (15s)**: "When we submit, our app calls the NVIDIA NIM API securely in the background, analyzing this specific behavior."
4. **Dashboard Reveal (1m)**: "Here's the dashboard. You can see the AI caught the DoorDash habit and created a specific spending alert. It built a savings roadmap. Notice the 'Explainer' card? It's teaching the user about the 'Pay Yourself First' concept, tailored specifically to their goal."
5. **Simulator (30s)**: "Finally, our Scenario Simulator. What if we cut dining out by 20%? The AI recalculates the roadmap instantly. It's practical, fast, and educational."
